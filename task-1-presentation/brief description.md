This infographic covers:

The basics of digital literacy:
The ability to find, evaluate, use, create, and communicate information effectively using digital technologies and the internet in a responsible manner.
Useful digital tools for students:
a. Google Docs – for creating and sharing documents
b. Khan Academy – for online learning and courses
c. Zoom – for virtual classes and meetings
Safe internet practices:
a. Use strong passwords to protect accounts
b. Protect personal information and avoid oversharing
c. Verify sources before trusting or sharing information

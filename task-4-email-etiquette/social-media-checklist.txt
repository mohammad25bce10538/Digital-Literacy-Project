Do's:
1. Think before you post: Make sure your content is appropriate and won’t harm your reputation.
2. Use privacy settings: Control who can see your posts and personal information.
3. Verify information: Check facts before sharing anything to avoid spreading misinformation.
4. Be respectful: Communicate politely and avoid arguments or offensive language.
5. Use it productively: Follow educational pages, join communities, and learn new skills.

Dont's:
1. Don’t share personal details: Avoid posting your address, phone number, or sensitive info.
2. Don’t engage in cyberbullying: Never post hurtful or harmful comments about others.
3. Don’t overshare: Posting everything about your life can affect privacy and safety.
4. Don’t believe everything online: Not all content is true or reliable.
5. Don’t waste excessive time: Avoid scrolling for hours and neglecting studies or responsibilities.

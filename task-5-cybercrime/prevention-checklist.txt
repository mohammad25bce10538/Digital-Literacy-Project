Checklist

Stay Safe Online Checklist:

1. Use different passwords for each account this will help you stay safe online.

2. Enable 2-factor authentication on apps this is a must for online safety.

3. Do not click on links or attachments they can be very harmful to your online safety.

4. Keep your phone and laptop updated regularly this is very important for safety.

5. Avoid using Wi-Fi for important logins it is not safe to do so.

6. Never share your OTP, passwords or personal details with anyone this is very important for staying online.

7. Remember that your UPI PIN is only used for sending money not receiving so be careful with your UPI PIN.

8. Always check the UPI ID or QR code carefully before making any payment this will help you avoid any mistakes.

9. Do not install screen sharing apps if a stranger asks you to it is not safe to do

Report here: cybercrime.gov.in

Helpline: 1930

Reflection

I was doing some research on safety and I found out that people can easily fall into online scams. This can happen even if they are not doing anything. What really surprised me was how normal these online scams look. A simple message or link can look completely real. If you are not careful you can lose money or access to your account in a few minutes.

One thing I am going to change is how I react to messages. I used to open messages without thinking much.. Now I know that I need to slow down and check things first. I will be more careful when I am making online payments using UPI. I will make sure I do not rush through them. This small change can make a difference, in staying safe online. I will remember to stay safe by following the Stay Safe Online Checklist.
